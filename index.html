The house

Light, calm and built around the view

Set on a green hillside on Ithaca, the house opens onto a wide terrace facing west across the Ionian. Inside, high vaulted ceilings, a soft powder-blue kitchen and cool stone floors keep it fresh through the Greek summer — a simple, comfortable base for a slow island holiday.

Recently finished throughout, it sleeps four across two bedrooms, with a fully equipped kitchen, fast Wi-Fi and everything you need for stays from a long weekend to a full season.

The island

Ithaca — the greener side of Greece

Odysseus's rugged little island in the Ionian is all pine-covered hills, hidden pebble coves and working fishing harbours. It is quieter and greener than the Cyclades — a place for swimming, slow lunches and boat days rather than crowds.

The main town of Vathi wraps around one of the Mediterranean's deepest natural harbours, while Kioni and Frikes to the north are postcard villages built for long tavern dinners by the water.
